Privacy Policy

Your trust matters. This policy explains how Wallpaper Gems collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app.

Summary

Wallpaper Gems is designed to deliver high‑quality wallpapers with a smooth, safe experience. We only collect the minimum data needed to operate the app, improve performance, and comply with platform policies.

No sale of personal data Child‑friendly compliance Limited analytics
Information We Collect
Device & App Data

We may collect basic device information (OS version, device model) and app usage events to understand performance and fix issues.

Crash & Diagnostics

If the app crashes, we may collect error logs to diagnose and improve stability.

Content Requests

When you browse or download wallpapers, we process those requests to deliver content and manage caching.

How We Use Information

We use collected data for the following purposes:

App Functionality

To deliver wallpapers, enable downloads, and optimize performance.

Security

To detect abuse, protect users, and maintain platform integrity.

Improvements

To understand usage patterns and improve features and UI.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited information with service providers that help us run the app (such as analytics or crash reporting tools). These providers are contractually required to protect your data and use it only for the agreed purpose.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

Data Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. After that, data is deleted or anonymized.

Your Choices

You can stop data collection by uninstalling the app. You may also contact us to request access to or deletion of any personal data we hold, subject to legal requirements.

Updates
